Fettuccine Alfredo Recipe

Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

An easy-to-make fettuccine alfredo recipe that requires minimal ingredients and tastes delicious at the same time. Inspired by a TikTok recipe that can be viewed here. If you're ready to make a quick and delicious meal, please read the recipe below!

Ingredients

  • 250g of uncooked pasta
  • 3 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1.5 tbsp flour
  • 1.5 cups milk
  • 1.5 cups cream (5% or 10%)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/8 cup of parsley
  • 1/2 cup shredded or grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup of pasta water

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in a pot for 8-10 minutes. Save some pasta water to add to sauce at the end.
  2. In another pot, melt 3 tbsp of unsalted butter on medium heat.
  3. Once the butter is melted, add 2 tbsp of minced garlic and 1.5 tbsp of flour, then stir until mixed.
  4. Add 1.5 cups of milk and 1.5 cups of 5% or 10% cream and stir.
  5. Add 1/2 tsp of salt, 1/2 tsp of pepper, 1/8 cup of parsley, and 1/2 cup of shredded or grated parmesan cheese, then stir.
  6. Let the sauce simmer on low to medium heat until it thickens and then add 1/4 cup of pasta water.
